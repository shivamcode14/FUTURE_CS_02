## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on identifying phishing emails and creating awareness to prevent cyber attacks.
Phishing is a common technique where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, OTPs, or banking details.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze phishing email samples
* Identify common phishing indicators
* Classify emails based on risk
* Explain phishing attacks in simple language
* Provide prevention and awareness guidelines

## 📧 Phishing Email Analysis

### 🔴 Example 1: Account Suspension Email

* **Type:** Account Verification Scam
* **Risk Level:** HIGH
* **Indicators:**

  * Fake link
  * Urgent message
  * Generic greeting

---

### 🔴 Example 2: Bank Fraud Email

* **Type:** Financial Scam
* **Risk Level:** HIGH
* **Indicators:**

  * Suspicious domain
  * Fear-based message
  * Request for sensitive information

---

## 🚨 Common Phishing Indicators

* Fake or suspicious sender email
* Urgency or threat messages
* Suspicious or shortened links
* Generic greetings (Dear User)
* Requests for OTP, password, or personal data

---

## ⚠️ Risk Classification

* 🟢 Safe
* 🟠 Suspicious
* 🔴 Phishing (High Risk)

---

## 🧠 How Phishing Attack Works

1. Attacker sends a fake email
2. Email creates urgency or fear
3. User clicks malicious link
4. Fake website opens
5. User enters sensitive data
6. Attacker steals information

---

## 🛡 Prevention Guidelines

### ✅ Do's

* Verify sender email address
* Check links before clicking
* Use official websites only
* Think before taking action

### ❌ Don'ts

* Do not click unknown links
* Do not share OTP or passwords
* Do not trust urgent emails blindly
* Do not download unknown attachments
